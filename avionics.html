Avionics

GLOBAL Procurement Services, Inc. is a broadside broker of aircraft avionics, parts equipment, instrumentation and systems specializing in Airplane on Ground (AOG).
We offer a complete line of aircraft avionics: Hardware/Electrical and Electronic Hardware and Systems/Hydraulics/Communication and Navigation Systems, Flight Control Systems/Weather/Collision Avoidance, Aircraft Management Systems/Radar and Sonar etc.
USA and Worldwide STOCK ready to be shipped immediately and depending on the circumstances or urgency we can ship directly from our sources.
We can provide repair and overhaul services directly from the manufacturers and/or authorized FAA facilities for any aircraft, engine, part or aircraft system, etc. at the best rates of the market.
Also We can supply New replacement parts direct from manufacturer or FAA authorized OEM's.
All our Quality Procedures are in accordance with ISO 9001:2008 with pendeing certification
Global Procurement Services, Inc. is registered with the USA department of State, to commercialize and export Strategic parts, systems and equipment under ITAR regulations. We are extreneky familiar with the filing licesing process that these products require, in a few words less delays and faster shipments.
