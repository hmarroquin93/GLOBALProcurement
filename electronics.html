Electronics

Gloval Procurement Services, Inc. is a broadside broker-distributor of Electronic Components (Active, Passive and Connectors) of commercial, industrial or military Grade, specializing in hard to find items due to their obsolescence or allocation.
We can offer USA Stock ready to be shipped, plus a Worldwide Network of Availabilities from manufactures, OEM's /CEM's and our approved Suppliers and Vendors. According to your urgency we can drop ship from our sources.
We expedite all the ordes with lead time. Pressing the manufacturers and following up for a faster delivery, keeping you informed of any possible changes in status.
Global Porcurement Services, Inc. also offers when possible to search for the right replacement for the obsolete or allocate parts at no extra cost, datasheets will be provided for your approval.
Quality Control
All of our procedures are in accordance with ISO 9001 :2008 with pending certification. At Global Procurement Services, Inc. we are deeply concerned about the nightmares that today are affecting our industry, just to name a few:
*Counterfeit Parts
*Defective lots that make their way into the distribution market
*Expired lots e.g. Time & Humidity sensitive materials improperly stored.....
*Used OTP's sold as EPROMS
There is no immediate solution to all these issues, only cooperation and understanding of the problems will help the industry to get a solution.
Our approach is to emphasize the inspection of the components/parts as soon as they are received and/or use Authorized Testing facilities in order to minimize any possible risk.
Our inspection procedure follows the parts from the moment they are received until they are shipped to our customer or rejected back to the supplier. We have a 50 point Inspection procedure to verify the components such as:
P/N accordina to the manufacturer specifications (thoroughly verification of: pin, packages. marking codes, containers and over all condition.) Global Procurement Services, Inc. has a "No Taking Chances Policy" Keeping our customers always informed about possible red flags that might come up from the inspections.
If something is not in accordance to our order or our customers order the materials will be rejected and we will take all the necessary measures in order to avoid that it doesn't happen again.
EXCESS INVENTORY We can list your excess inventory for you and sell the items as you please (whole or partial) with the biggest returns for your company.
We can also offer you the consignment alternative where your company will get the bigger returns in which Global Procurement Services, Inc. will be selling your inventory throughout the years as the market requires your parts.
Your inventory will be listed in our Worldwide Network of Availabilities in order to provide a wider exposure.
We also can promote or market some particular items that you might have special interest.
Please let us know, what is more suitable for your operations according to your necessities at any particular moment.
